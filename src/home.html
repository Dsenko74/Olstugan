Сulture passed down through generations

Ölstugan Tullen opened its first pub at the street Andra Långgatan, today there are seven Ölstugan Tullen pubs around Gothenburg. When Ölstugan opened at the street Andra Långgatan in 2010, it took its name and inspiration from an old drinking establishment located in the area in the late 19th century.

The new Ölstugen concept was a huge success and today there are seven Tullen pubs around Gothenburg. They all exclusively sell Swedish beer, and serve traditional Swedish food. You’ll find plenty of Gothenburg breweries represented here.
